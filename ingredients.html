Penny Juice Natural Ingredients

Our juices are made with 100% real fruit juice concentrates and purees, blended with care to keep things fresh, flavorful, and full of life. No artificial colors, sweeteners, or preservatives—ever.

Core Fruit Ingredients

  • Green Apple– crisp, tart, hydrating
  • Kiwi– tangy, vitamin-rich, fiber-packed
  • Pear– mellow sweetness, high in antioxidants
  • Mango– tropical, juicy, rich in vitamins A and C
  • Yellow Lemon– zesty, cleansing, immune-boosting
  • Pineapple– bright, sweet-tart, packed with bromelain

Other Natural Additions

  • Filtered Water – for a smooth, refreshing base
  • Agave or Coconut Nectar – optional light natural sweetener
  • Lime Juice – for added brightness and alkalizing effects
  • Mint Leaves – cooling and soothing
  • Ginger Extract – for a spicy kick and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Turmeric Root – a powerful antioxidant and immunity booster
